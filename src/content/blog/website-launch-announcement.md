## Our Journey: 17+ Years of Excellence

Let us take you back to where it all began.

### 2007: The Foundation

DPM Enterprise was founded with a simple vision: to create spaces where people thrive. Starting with a small team and big dreams, we took on our first residential project in Mumbai.

### 2010-2015: Growth & Recognition

As word spread about our quality work and client-first approach, we expanded into corporate interiors and government projects. We earned our ISO 9001:2015 certification, validating our commitment to quality management.

### 2016-2020: Manufacturing Excellence

We established our own manufacturing facility in Virar, giving us complete control over quality and timelines. This vertical integration became a key differentiator, allowing us to offer competitive pricing without compromising on quality.

### 2021-Present: Industry Leadership

Today, we're proud to be:
- **ISO 9001:2015 Certified** for quality management
- **MSME Registered** supporting India's manufacturing sector
- **GeM Portal Registered** for government procurement
- **Startup India Recognized** for innovation and growth

We've served prestigious clients across sectors including Defense, Hospitality, Corporate, and Government institutions.
